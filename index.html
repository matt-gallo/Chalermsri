Our story

From the mountain to your cup.

Chalermsri began as a small intention on Doi Pa Miang Mae Sai — a family coffee farm tended slowly, season after season, from cherry to roast.

Today we hand-roast organic beans freshly in the shop, right beside Phra That Doi Saket. You’ll often see them drying in the sun by the door. Choose a blend to suit your taste, or take a bag home.
